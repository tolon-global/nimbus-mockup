50%
  1. Open the top cover of the machine
  2. Unscrew the ESB cable terminals from the ESB contact socket
  3. Remove the contact of the ESB from its back by using a screwdriver
  4. Unbolt the plastic nut from the back of the ESB
  5. Pull the ESB from the front of the CP
  6. Put a new ESB into the empty ESB slot

  10. Close the top cover of the machine
